The Coffee Compass
Find your way to a better-tasting filter brew
Extract Less, Use More Coffee
  • Use a coarser grind and/or shorter brew time to extract more
  • Decrease the Brew Ratio by fixing the water weight and using more coffee OR by fixing the dose and using less water
How to Start
  • Brew a coffee and taste it. Make a note of any negative flavours you find.
  • Click and drag the compass wheel to rotate it until you find the flavours you taste in your coffee.
How to Start
  • Once you've chosen a flavour, suggestions for improving your brew appear below.
  • Following these suggestions will help you get to the centre of the compass.
Search
  • If you can't find your flavour on the wheel, try clicking the search button to find it. There are a lot more flavours than fit on the wheel!
  • The wheel will show you the flavour most similar to the one you search for.
  • If you can't find the flavour you're looking for, let us know in the comments below and we can add it to the app.
